Rolena Adorno: The Nueva corónica y buen gobierno.

A New Look at the Royal Library’s Peruvian Treasure

in: Fund og Forskning 24 (1979-80): pp. 7-28

After the digitization of theNueva corónica y buen gobiernomanuscript, I planned to update my 1979-80 article with a series of addenda. Such corrections and modifications were quickly transcended and gradually grew into a fully independent inquiry. The new essay, entitled “A Witness unto Itself: The Integrity of the Autograph Manuscript of Felipe Guaman Poma de Ayala's Nueva corónica y buen gobierno,” is published on this website. It will also appear in print in Fund og Forskning 2002.

While most of the contents of my 1979-80 essay are still current, readers should refer to “A Witness unto Itself” regarding: (1) quires, watermarks, and bindings of the manuscript; (2) the date of its composition; (3) the possibility of a missing chapter; (4) the 1936 facsimile reproduction of the manuscript; and (5) the biography of Guaman Poma. See also the Royal Library’s 2002 systematic codicological survey of the manuscript on this website.
