Guaman Poma: El primer Nueva corónica y buen gobierno


Table of contents


By John Charles and Rolena Adorno


This table of contents is intended to provide a guide to the prose text of the Nueva corónica y buen gobierno. To facilitate its use, we have prepared chapter headings and subentries, which are generally consonant with Guaman Poma's original division of his work. Those readers interested primarily in Guaman Poma’s visual art may consult the table of drawings that we have prepared. (We use there the same chapter headings that we developed here.) The reader may also consult Guaman Poma’s “table of the said chronicle,” located at the end of the autograph manuscript (1179-1187). The page numbers he employed, however, correspond to his original, flawed pagination, which has been corrected throughout the Siglo XXI edition and in this one, prepared for the Internet.
